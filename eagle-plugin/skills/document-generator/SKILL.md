## Document 1: Statement of Work (SOW)

### Purpose
Defines the work to be performed, deliverables, and performance requirements.

### Required Inputs
- Title/description of requirement
- Period of performance
- Place of performance
- Tasks and deliverables
- Quality requirements
- Government-furnished items (if any)

### Template Structure

```markdown
# STATEMENT OF WORK (SOW)
## [Title]
### National Cancer Institute (NCI)

**Document Status:** DRAFT — Generated [Date]

---

## 1. BACKGROUND AND PURPOSE

The National Cancer Institute (NCI), part of the National Institutes of Health (NIH),
requires [description]. This Statement of Work (SOW) describes the tasks, deliverables,
and performance requirements for this acquisition.

## 2. SCOPE

The contractor shall provide all personnel, equipment, supplies, facilities,
transportation, tools, materials, supervision, and other items and non-personal
services necessary to [accomplish the objective].

## 3. PERIOD OF PERFORMANCE

[Period - typically 12 months from date of award, with options]

## 4. APPLICABLE DOCUMENTS AND STANDARDS

- Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR)
- Health and Human Services Acquisition Regulation (HHSAR)
- FAR 52.212-4 Contract Terms and Conditions — Commercial Products/Services
- Section 508 Accessibility Standards (if applicable)
- [Additional standards based on requirement type]

## 5. TASKS AND REQUIREMENTS

### Task 1: [Task Name]
[Description of task, acceptance criteria, deliverables]

### Task 2: [Task Name]
[Description of task, acceptance criteria, deliverables]

[Additional tasks as needed]

## 6. DELIVERABLES

| Deliverable | Due Date | Format |
|-------------|----------|--------|
| Project Management Plan | 30 days after award | PDF/Word |
| Monthly Status Reports | 5th of each month | PDF |
| Final Report | 30 days before end | PDF |

## 7. GOVERNMENT-FURNISHED PROPERTY (GFP)

[List items provided by the Government, or "None required"]

## 8. PLACE OF PERFORMANCE

[Primary: NCI/NIH Bethesda, MD 20892]
[Or contractor facility, or mix]

## 9. SECURITY REQUIREMENTS

[Based on data sensitivity and access requirements]
[HHSAR requirements if applicable]

## 10. QUALITY ASSURANCE SURVEILLANCE PLAN (QASP)

Performance will be evaluated using the following criteria:
| Performance Standard | AQL | Method |
|---------------------|-----|--------|
| [Standard] | [%] | [Inspection/Review] |

---

```

### Generation Best Practices
- Use **performance specifications** rather than design specifications
- State requirements in terms of **functions and outcomes**
- Include **measurable acceptance criteria**
- Avoid overly prescriptive requirements that limit competition
- Reference applicable industry standards (ISO, IEEE, etc.)

## Document 2: Independent Government Cost Estimate (IGCE)

### Purpose
Provides the Government's independent estimate of anticipated costs for budget planning and price reasonableness determination.

### Required Inputs
- Title/description
- Line items with quantities and unit costs
- Labor categories and rates (if applicable)
- Overhead/indirect cost assumptions
- Period of performance

### Template Structure

```markdown

## [Title]
### National Cancer Institute (NCI)

**Prepared:** [Date]
**Document Status:** DRAFT — For Budget Planning Purposes

---

This Independent Government Cost Estimate (IGCE) provides a detailed estimate
of the anticipated costs for: [title].
```

### IGCE Best Practices
- **Develop independently** - Don't base on contractor estimates alone
- **Document sources** - All pricing should be traceable
- **Include contingency** - 5-15% for unforeseen requirements
- **Update regularly** - IGCE should reflect current market conditions
- **Consider total cost** - Include training, maintenance, installation

## Generation Workflow

1. **Receive request** with document type and context
2. **Validate inputs** - Check for required fields
3. **Load template** from `data/templates/`
4. **Populate fields** from intake context and user inputs
5. **Apply business logic** - Thresholds, requirements, calculations
6. **Generate document** with proper formatting
7. **Save to S3** - `eagle/{tenant}/generated/{intake_id}/{doc_type}.md`
8. **Return confirmation** with document preview
