## Required Sections
1. HEADER — Subject, date, contract/solicitation number, contracting officer, applicable FAR/HHSAR citation
2. STATUTORY OR REGULATORY AUTHORITY — Cite the specific FAR / HHSAR / agency policy section requiring or authorizing the D&F (e.g., FAR 6.302-1 sole source, FAR 16.301-2 cost-reimbursement, FAR 12.207 commercial item)
3. DESCRIPTION OF ACTION — What action is being justified (sole source, contract type, deviation, etc.)
4. FINDINGS — Substantive paragraphs establishing the facts that support the determination. Each finding must be tied to evidence (market research, technical analysis, prior history)

6. APPROVAL — Signature block. Approval level depends on dollar value and authority (CO, BCO, HCO, HCA per FAR 1.704)
