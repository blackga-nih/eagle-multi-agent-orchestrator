## Required Sections
1. LETTERHEAD — NCI/NIH letterhead block, date, addressee (COR name and organizational title)
2. SUBJECT LINE — *"Designation as Contracting Officer's Representative — Contract [Number]"*
3. DESIGNATION STATEMENT — Pursuant to FAR 1.602-2(d), I hereby designate you as my Contracting Officer's Representative for Contract [Number] effective [date]
4. CONTRACT INFORMATION — Contract number, contractor name, period of performance, total estimated value, contract type
5. AUTHORIZED DUTIES — Specific delegated duties:
   - Technical direction within scope
   - Inspection and acceptance of deliverables
   - Review of invoices for technical accuracy
   - Performance monitoring and CPARS input
   - Coordination of meetings and site visits
   - Preparation of the contract file
6. LIMITATIONS — Express prohibition on actions reserved to the CO:
   - No commitment, change, or modification of the contract terms (price, schedule, scope)
   - No authorization of cost overruns or out-of-scope work
   - No contractual direction outside the SOW/PWS
   - No release of unauthorized information
7. FAC-COR LEVEL REQUIREMENT — Confirm the COR holds the appropriate FAC-COR Level (I, II, or III) for this contract complexity; reference Certification document
8. REPORTING — Frequency of status reports to CO; immediate notification of issues (cost growth, schedule slip, performance failure, fraud indicators)
9. REVOCATION — Statement that this designation is revocable at any time by the CO; designation terminates upon contract closeout, COR reassignment, or written revocation
10. ACKNOWLEDGEMENT — COR signature block acknowledging acceptance and understanding of duties and limitations
11. SIGNATURE — Contracting Officer signature block; cc: COR supervisor, contract file
