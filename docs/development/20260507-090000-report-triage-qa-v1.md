## Executive Summary

Bedrock `ServiceUnavailableException` caused a sustained ~90-minute outage window for the primary model (`us.anthropic.claude-sonnet-4-6`) in QA between 08:12–09:45 UTC on 2026-05-07. The circuit breaker correctly cascaded traffic to fallback models (Sonnet 4.5/Haiku), but a failure counter leak in `ModelCircuitBreaker` causes the counter to climb indefinitely (2 to 11) across OPEN/HALF_OPEN cycles without resetting. No user feedback was submitted and no frontend errors were logged, suggesting either no active QA users during the window or the cascade masked the outage. Zero DynamoDB feedback items exist for the `default-qa` tenant.
