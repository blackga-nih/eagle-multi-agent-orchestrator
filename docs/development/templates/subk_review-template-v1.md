# Subcontracting Plan Review — Template

**Purpose**: The Subcontracting Plan Review is the contracting officer's written review of an offeror's or contractor's small business subcontracting plan against the required elements of FAR 52.219-9. It documents whether each required element is present and adequate, evaluates the reasonableness of proposed socioeconomic goals against agency prior-year achievement and industry benchmarks, captures any deficiencies requiring revision, records SBA Procurement Center Representative coordination when applicable, and produces a final approve / approve-subject-to-revisions / return / reject recommendation prior to award or plan acceptance.

**Authority**: FAR 19.705-4 (Reviewing the Subcontracting Plan), FAR 52.219-9 (Small Business Subcontracting Plan), HHSAR 352.219 (HHS supplemental subcontracting policy), SBA PCR review per FAR 19.705-4(d) when applicable.

**Audience**: Contracting Officer (reviewer of record), Small Business Specialist (concurrence), SBA Procurement Center Representative (when applicable per FAR 19.705-4(d)), Source Selection Authority (pre-award review).

You are an NCI federal acquisition specialist. Generate a Subcontracting Plan Review memorandum in markdown format. This is the contracting officer's written review of a subcontracting plan submitted by an offeror or contractor per FAR 19.705-4 and FAR 52.219-9.

## Required Sections
1. MEMO HEADER — Date, contractor name, solicitation/contract number, plan version reviewed, NAICS code, total contract value, reviewing contracting officer
2. PLAN TYPE — Individual / Master / Commercial (per FAR 19.704) with one-line justification of why the submitted type is appropriate
3. ADEQUACY REVIEW CHECKLIST — 10-row table covering each FAR 52.219-9 required element (Goals, Description of subcontracted products/services, Method for developing goals, Indirect costs treatment, Small Business Liaison Officer, Outreach / good-faith efforts, FAR 52.219-9 flow-down commitment, ISR/SSR reporting via eSRS, Record keeping, Authorized signature) with Present? / Adequate? / Reviewer Notes columns
4. GOAL REASONABLENESS ANALYSIS — Substantive discussion comparing proposed SB / SDB / WOSB / HUBZone / VOSB / SDVOSB percentages against the agency's prior-year achievement, the relevant industry benchmark, and the subcontractable portion of work
5. FINDINGS / DEFICIENCIES — Itemized numbered list of each deficiency requiring revision, tied to the specific FAR 52.219-9 element it fails — not a summary paragraph
6. SBA PCR REVIEW — Statement of whether the SBA Procurement Center Representative review is required per FAR 19.705-4(d), date submitted, date returned, and SBA position
7. RECOMMENDATION — Approve as submitted / Approve subject to revisions / Return for revision / Reject — with substantive rationale tied to the Findings section
8. SIGNATURE — Contracting officer name, title, date; cc: Small Business Specialist, contract file

## Rules
- Every FAR 52.219-9 required element must be addressed in the checklist — do not skip rows or roll multiple elements into one entry
- Goal reasonableness must be measured against BOTH the agency's prior-year achievement AND the relevant industry benchmark — citing only one is insufficient
- Deficiencies must be itemized in the Findings section and tied to the specific FAR 52.219-9 element they fail — do not summarize ("plan needs work") in lieu of an itemized list
- The recommendation (approve / approve subject to revisions / return / reject) requires substantive rationale referencing the Findings — boilerplate approval language is insufficient
- SBA PCR review status must be explicitly recorded when the contract is subject to FAR 19.705-4(d); if not applicable, state "Not applicable — [reason]"
- If information is missing, write "[Contracting Officer to complete: <what's needed>]"
- Do NOT paste raw user messages or chat responses into the document
