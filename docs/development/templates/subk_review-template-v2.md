# Subcontracting Plan Review — Template (v2 — KB-grounded)

**Purpose**: The Subcontracting Plan Review documents the contracting officer's pre-award review of an offeror's small business subcontracting plan against the 15 required elements of FAR 52.219-9(d), captures the OSDBU Small Business Specialist (SBS) and SBA Procurement Center Representative (PCR) advisory positions, records the three CO determinations required on the HHS OSDBU Subcontracting Plan Review Form, and produces a final approve / approve-subject-to-revisions / return / reject recommendation. Per the **HHS Subcontracting Plan Review Process SOP** (sourced from the HHS OSDBU Subcontracting Plan Review Form, Rev. April 2017; revised April 2026 for HHS FAR Class Deviation 2026-01 and AA 2023-02 Amendment 4), this is **Process 2** (Pre-Award, Post-Proposal) and is distinct from the Process 1 HHS-653 Pre-Solicitation review.

**Authority**: FAR 19.705-4 (codified) / RFO FAR 19.302-2 (operative for HHS per HHS FAR CD 2026-01); FAR 52.219-9 (Small Business Subcontracting Plan, 15 elements at (d)(1)–(d)(15)); FAR 52.219-16 (Liquidated Damages); HHSAR 352.219-70 (Mentor-Protégé Program provision, required by HHSAR 319.270-1); HHS Subcontracting Plan Review Process SOP (HHS OSDBU Subcontracting Plan Review Form, Rev. April 2017).

**Audience**: Contracting Officer (Step 1 reviewer of record); OSDBU Small Business Specialist (Step 2, advisory); SBA Procurement Center Representative (Step 3, advisory with strong weight per SOP); contract file. **All three reviewers must sign before contract award** (HHS SOP).

You are an NCI federal acquisition specialist. Generate a Subcontracting Plan Review memorandum in markdown format. This is the contracting officer's written Step 1 review of a non-small offeror's plan submitted on a contract expected to exceed $900,000 ($2M for construction) per FAR 52.219-9 / RFO FAR 19.109.

## Required Sections
1. MEMO HEADER — Date, contractor, solicitation/contract number, plan version, NAICS, total contract value, reviewing CO; flag whether HHSAR 352.219-70 was included in the solicitation per HHSAR 319.270-1
2. PLAN TYPE — Individual / Master + Individual Addendum / Commercial (FAR 19.704). If "Master" is checked, "Individual" addendum must ALSO be checked and both documents submitted (per SOP).
3. DOCUMENTATION PACKAGE RECEIVED — Confirm the four-item package required by the SOP: (a) signed HHS Subcontracting Plan Review Form, (b) signed plan with all 15 elements, (c) Summary of Proposed Costs from Final Proposal Revision, (d) supplemental documentation (small-business sources narrative, rationale for using other-than-small, market research)
4. ADEQUACY REVIEW CHECKLIST — 15-row table covering each FAR 52.219-9(d)(1)–(d)(15) element (see worked example) with Present? / Adequate? / Reviewer Notes columns
5. GOAL REASONABLENESS ANALYSIS — Goals must cover **six** SB socioeconomic categories (SB, VOSB, SDVOSB, HUBZone, SDB, WOSB) expressed as BOTH dollars AND percentage of total subcontracting dollars. The "other than small business" line on the HHS form is OSDBU tracking, NOT a FAR goal. Compare proposed goals to NCI prior-year achievement and the subcontractable scope estimate.
6. PAST PERFORMANCE CONSIDERATION — Per FAR 19.705-4 / RFO 19.302-2: review of prior eSRS reports, ISR/SSR submission history, prior good-faith effort indicators
7. CO DETERMINATIONS (THREE, per SOP) — Yes/No on each: (a) Plan meets FAR 19.704 requirements; (b) Past performance considered; (c) Additional pre-award review required
8. STEP 2 — OSDBU SMALL BUSINESS SPECIALIST POSITION — Date submitted, date returned, SBS recommendation (acceptance / rejection), SBS signature
9. STEP 3 — SBA PCR POSITION — Date submitted, date returned, concur / non-concur, signature on review form AND plan signature page (both required by SOP)
10. FINDINGS / DEFICIENCIES — Itemized; tied to the specific (d)(1)–(d)(15) element that fails. Common issues per SOP: goals too low, methodology deficient, indirect-cost statement unclear, administrator undefined, FAR 52.219-8 flowdown missing.
11. RECOMMENDATION — Approve / Approve subject to revisions / Return for revision / Reject. If contractor refuses to correct deficiencies, plan is unacceptable and award cannot be made (SOP).
12. SIGNATURE — CO name, title, date; cc: OSDBU SBS, SBA PCR, contract file

## Rules
- The **HHS Subcontracting Plan Review Process SOP** is the canonical authority for HHS reviews; where it differs from generic FAR 19.705-4 guidance, the SOP wins.
- Threshold per SOP: plan required when contract is expected to exceed **$900,000** ($2M construction), contractor is other than small, and subcontracting possibilities exist. Below threshold = no formal plan; if no subcontracting possibilities, CO must prepare detailed rationale approved at level above CO and place in contract file.
- Address all **15** FAR 52.219-9(d)(1)–(d)(15) elements — do not collapse rows. (Prior 10-element checklists are non-compliant.)
- Goals must cover the **six** SB socioeconomic categories from FAR 52.219-9(d)(1). Do **not** present "other than small business" as a goal — per SOP it is OSDBU tracking only.
- For "Master" plan type, both the master plan AND a contract-specific individual addendum must be submitted; check both boxes.
- (d)(9) requires **two distinct flowdown assurances** (52.219-8 for all subs offering further subcontracting; 52.219-9 for non-small subs >$900K). Missing 52.219-8 is the most common deficiency per SOP.
- Build **10–15 business days** into the evaluation schedule for the three-step review (CO → OSDBU SBS → SBA PCR). All three signatures are required before award.
- SBA PCR review is **NOT required** for: total small business set-asides, FAR 8.002/8.003 orders, actions with approved JOFOC (FAR 6.3) or LSJ (FAR 8.405-6), or SBIR contracts. Document the exemption explicitly when claimed.
- Deficiency notification: CO works with contractor to correct before award. If contractor refuses, plan is unacceptable and contractor is ineligible for award (SOP). Recommendation field must reflect this when applicable.
- Confirm solicitation included FAR 52.219-9, FAR 52.219-16 (liquidated damages), AND HHSAR 352.219-70 (Mentor-Protégé provision per HHSAR 319.270-1) — flag in header if any are missing.
- Past performance per FAR 19.705-4 / RFO 19.302-2: prior failure to meet goals without good-faith effort, missing ISR/SSR submissions, or payment issues are material findings.
- Itemize deficiencies tied to the specific (d) element; do not summarize. Recommendation must reference Findings — boilerplate approval is insufficient.
- If information is missing, write "[Contracting Officer to complete: <what's needed>]".
- Do NOT paste raw user messages or chat responses into the document.
- Include "DRAFT — Generated {date}" in header metadata where {date} is today's date.
- End with: *This document was generated by EAGLE — NCI Acquisition Assistant*
